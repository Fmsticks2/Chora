Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers

Is ChoraAI secure?

+

Yes, ChoraAI is built on the Internet Computer Protocol (ICP), which provides enterprise-grade security. All transactions are cryptographically secured, and we never store your private keys. Your funds remain in your control at all times.

Which blockchains are supported?

+

ChoraAI currently supports Bitcoin, Ethereum, Internet Computer (ICP), and several other major blockchains. Our Chain Fusion technology enables seamless cross-chain operations without the need for bridges or wrapped tokens.

How accurate is the AI?

+

Our AI maintains an average accuracy rate of 85-90% for market predictions. However, remember that all trading involves risk, and past performance doesn't guarantee future results. Always do your own research and never invest more than you can afford to lose.

What are the fees?

+

ChoraAI charges a competitive 0.25% trading fee on executed orders. There are no subscription fees for basic features. Premium features like advanced AI strategies may have additional costs, which will be clearly disclosed.

Can I use ChoraAI on mobile?

+

Yes! ChoraAI is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices. We're also developing dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android, which will be available soon.
